Description

Diceworld is a streamlined 3D dice roller with exciting character physics reactions and varied themed worlds. It makes complicated roles easy, featuring a comprehensive spell creation and spellbook system for DnD 5E (with many other tabletop systems supported). Diceworld is much more than a roll calculator, allowing players to enjoy every part of the rolling experience. Complicated rolls can also be quickly resolved and clearly breakdown damage types to take the hassle out of casting epic spells so players can get right back to the action.

History

Diceworld's team are long-time tabletop fans and saw an opportunity to liven up dice rollers with our unique take on the dice rolling genre. We've battle tested Diceworld at our gaming tables and are excited to share it with new and experienced players alike across many different tabletop games.

Features

  • 7 polyhedral Dice for pick-up-and-play in the world's best tabletop roleplaying games
  • 11 colourful Worlds each with their own music, characters and hidden secrets
  • 9 fully customisable Dice Skins, each made of a different alluring material
  • 40+ dynamic character Minis that ragdoll on collision including: Heroes, Animals, Titans, and Dragons
  • Customisable Roll Presets, for those complex attacks, spells, and damage rolls
  • Pre-loaded with 100's of the most common spells available in DnD 5E
  • Screenshot mode, with customisable shading settings
  • Proudly Ad free
