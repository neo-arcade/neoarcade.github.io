Diceworld

Forgot your dice? New to tabletop RPGs? We've got you covered!

Diceworld makes all your dice rolls fun and simple to set up. No more messing with complicated calculations or waiting an age for your wizard to finish rolling that epic spell.

Keep the action moving with a wide range of fantasy minis in glorious slow motion, and let us handle the math.

Featuring:
  • 7 polyhedral Dice for pick-up-and-play in the world's best tabletop roleplaying games
  • 11 colourful Worlds each with their own music, characters and hidden secrets
  • 9 fully customisable Dice Skins, each made of a different alluring material
  • 40+ dynamic character Minis that ragdoll on collision including: Heroes, Animals, Titans, and Dragons
  • Customisable Roll Presets, for those complex attacks, spells, and damage rolls
  • Pre-loaded with 100's of the most common spells available in DnD 5E
  • Screenshot mode, with customisable shading settings
  • Proudly Ad free

Made for tabletop RPG and board game newcomers and diehards alike. We hope Diceworld adds as much to your game table as it has to ours!

Happy rolling!
-The Neo Arcade team
